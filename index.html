Chenyu (Sev) Hou | 侯宸羽

Ph.D Candidate | UBC

I’m a PhD Candidate from Vancouver School of Economics, University of British Columbia.

My research interest is in Applied Macroeconomics, with specific focus on expectation formation and learning behaviours.
I also have special interest on utilizing Deep Learning tools to uncover non-linear behaviours in agents’ expectation formation process as well as solving projection problems with high-dimensional input.

I obtained Master of Science in Economics from University of Texas Austin and Master of Arts from Simon Fraser University.
Before coming to North America, I graduated from Fudan University, Shanghai, China with Bachelor of Arts
